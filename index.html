About
Background

Civil engineer by training. I started in civil engineering, moved into real estate development, and now serve as PMO Director at Zerra DC in Singapore.

The thread across all of it: I build systems that give people clarity — whether that's a geospatial platform that shows you where to build, an AI pipeline that writes your market research, or a project management framework that tells leadership exactly where things stand.

I work across the full stack: Python for automation and data, React for interfaces, QGIS for spatial analysis, and the Claude API for intelligent agents. I write code to solve operational problems, not as an end in itself.
